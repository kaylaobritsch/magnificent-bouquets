Welcome

Magnificent Bouquets flowers travel far and wide, from our studio based in Oadby, Leicestershire, bringing comfort and delight to those who give and receive them.

Renowned for luxurious hand-tied bouquets, sumptuous wedding flowers and spectacular venue dressings for special events, Magnificent Bouquets create floral designs that always stand out from the crowd. Why not make a statement with a Magnificent Bouquet?

Services

So every job is approached with creative flair to produce flower arrangements that are eye-catching, structural, exotic and truly beautiful. Magnificent Bouquets now bring colour to weddings, funerals and special events throughout the East Midlands and beyond.
